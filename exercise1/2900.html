The Future in 2900

By the year 2900, humanity has achieved galactic civilization, with colonies on distant planets and moons. Quantum computers control vast portions of the economy, and human consciousness can be uploaded into virtual worlds. Despite the technological marvels, many wonder if humanity has lost something essential in the process.

Notable Developments:

  • Colonization of over 100 star systems
  • Consciousness uploading and digital immortality
  • Discovery of intelligent alien life
