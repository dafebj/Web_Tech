Events of the Early 20th Century

The year 1900 is a time of great change. The world is on the brink of technological revolutions, with the rise of electricity, automobiles, and the telephone. Meanwhile, imperial powers are vying for control across the globe, setting the stage for the major conflicts of the century.

Key Historical Events:

  • First powered flight by the Wright brothers
  • The discovery of the electron
  • The start of the Industrial Revolution's second phase
